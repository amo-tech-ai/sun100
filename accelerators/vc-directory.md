## Application Tips

### Before Reaching Out

1. **Research thoroughly** — Understand each VC's AI focus, portfolio, and investment thesis
2. **Check stage fit** — Verify they invest at your stage (pre-seed, seed, Series A, etc.)
3. **Prepare materials** — Pitch deck, demo, traction metrics, team bios
4. **Network** — Connect with portfolio companies and get warm introductions

### Pitch Best Practices

1. **Show AI differentiation** — Demonstrate unique AI/ML approach or technology
2. **Highlight technical team** — Emphasize AI/ML expertise and research background
3. **Show traction** — Demonstrate market validation, revenue, or user growth
4. **Be specific** — Explain how the VC's network/resources will help your AI startup
5. **Follow up** — Track engagement and respond promptly to requests

### Using Trackable Pitch Decks

- Use secure, trackable links (e.g., Papermark) instead of email attachments
- Track investor engagement with your AI pitch deck
- Get notified when VCs view your materials
- Protect your IP with watermarking and access controls
