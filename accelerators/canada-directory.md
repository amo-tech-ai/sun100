## General & Tech Accelerators

### Creative Destruction Lab (CDL)

- **Website:** [creativedestructionlab.com](https://www.creativedestructionlab.com/)
- **Type:** Accelerator
- **Specialization:** Science and technology-focused startups, deep-tech, science-based companies, **AI Stream** (specific stream for AI ventures)
- **Funding Provided:** $100K+ for 7% equity (varies by stream)
- **Equity Terms:** Non-dilutive (no equity taken from program side; investment deals negotiated externally)
- **Program Duration:** ~9 months per stream
- **Location:** Multiple cities across Canada (Toronto, Montreal, Vancouver, Calgary) + global outreach
- **Stages:** Pre-seed, seed
- **Benefits:**
  - Milestone-based acceleration
  - Mentorship from scientists/entrepreneurs
  - Investor network access
  - Structured milestone-based program
  - Network of investors and corporate partners
  - Connects founders with seasoned entrepreneurs, investors, and scientists
- **Application:** Annual cycles — check site 2026 schedule
- **Success Rate:** 85% (based on PitchBook alumni outcomes)
- **Additional Info:** Best for deep-tech founders aiming for high-impact research→product ventures. Focuses on scalable, science-based companies including AI/ML, quantum, biotech, deep-tech. **CDL AI Stream** is a highly-regarded deep-tech accelerator with a specific stream for AI ventures. **CDL alumni have created $95B+ in equity value.** Unique for being non-dilutive and connecting founders with a network to help build commercially viable products. Consistently ranked among top accelerators globally.

### DMZ (Toronto Metropolitan University)

- **Website:** [dmz.torontomu.ca](https://dmz.torontomu.ca/)
- **Type:** Accelerator/Incubator
- **Specialization:** Technology startups, SaaS, software, tech-enabled products
- **Funding Provided:** $50K for 5% equity (incubator track: 2.5% common shares)
- **Equity Terms:** 5% equity (standard) or 2.5% (incubator track)
- **Program Duration:** 4 months (accelerator) or up to 18 months (incubator track)
- **Location:** Toronto, Ontario, Canada
- **Stages:** Pre-seed, seed
- **Benefits:**
  - Mentorship & expert support
  - Workspace & startup community
  - Investor-matching & fundraising help
  - Tech-partner credits & services
  - Access to Toronto startup ecosystem
- **Application:** Two cohorts per year — check site for 2026 intake
- **Additional Info:** Top-ranked Canadian incubator; good general support but AI-specialized resources may vary. One of the world's top university-affiliated incubators.

## Application Tips

### Before Applying

1. **Research thoroughly** — Understand each accelerator's focus, track record, and requirements
2. **Check eligibility** — Verify stage, industry, and location requirements
3. **Prepare materials** — Pitch deck, business plan, team bios, traction metrics
4. **Network** — Connect with alumni and mentors before applying

### Application Best Practices

1. **Tailor your application** — Highlight alignment with accelerator's focus
2. **Show traction** — Demonstrate market validation, revenue, or user growth
3. **Emphasize team** — Highlight technical expertise and complementary skills
4. **Be specific** — Explain how the accelerator's resources will help your startup
5. **Follow up** — Track application status and respond promptly to requests

### Using Trackable Pitch Decks

- Use secure, trackable links (e.g., Papermark) instead of email attachments
- Track investor/accelerator engagement with your deck
- Get notified when accelerators view your materials
- Protect your IP with watermarking and access controls
